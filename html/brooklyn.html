On The
Bridge

A fun-filled interactive experience to the visitors on the Brooklyn Bridge and, to prevent pedestrians and cyclists from colliding.

This is an annual report which presents full details about the Taipei International Airport, including its staff, events, and activities in 2013. This work includes the hard print and the electric version (by using InDesign DPS) for mobile devices with interaction.

The concept of this project is to show a clean and bright vision that represents the airport. The concept of color gradients (blue, purple, light brown) that used on the cover design is to connect the sky and ground together. The elements I used was a world map (dotted style), and an airplane icon combining with the logotype.

YEAR 2013

MEDIUM mobile, paper

TOOLS Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign

CLINET Song-Shan Airport - Taipei Int. Airport
