Taiwan
Cuisine

The mobile app concept that based on the serie of illustrations to showcase the delightfulness of the Taiwanese cuisines.

This mobile app concept is based on the serie of illustrations that I created for the practicing purpose. I want to introduce several special Taiwan cuisines recipes to the world with simple techniques, basic ideas and methods.

Red colors in Taiwan represent a sense of happiness. I used pink-orange as a background and basic tone of the design to show a warm and relaxing atmosphere. The Recipe begins with a list of dishes that people can choose what they like inorder to learn how to cook it. Then the introduction of ingredients with specific volumes that should be prepared for the cooking. After the prepare steps (choping, washing, or seasoning...etc), the cooking steps are very important, as the fire should be controled carefully, and the timing of which ingredient should be put into the pan...

The app present a colorful feature among dishes and ingredients, which can give users a sense of joy while following the steps through the making process.
