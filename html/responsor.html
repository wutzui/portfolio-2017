MEDIA + SOCIETY

Responsor
App

An app that offers services for sponsors to build more tangible connections with their sponsored kids.

Child sponsorship is a powerful way to help one poor child break the bonds of poverty. It connects the sponsor with an individual child who desperately needs help, and lets you provide life-changing benefits to that child for a low monthly contribution. It sounds like a marketing pitch, and lots of people feel skeptical about it, but it actually works effectively.

I consider the platform can be applied to both web and mobile devices. Now I just make it into a mobile version to have a quick prototype.

Research

Why sponsorship?

The study shows that people are more willing to give their money to the specific person in need. And the relationship may last if the connection is frequent and tangible, which makes the child sponsorship successful. source link

How it works?

sponsor:
1. Donate amount.
2. Get child's information.
3. Send gifts or mails.
4. Receive updates.
5. plan for a visit.
child:
1. NGO reach out.
2. Get help.
3. Receive packages.
4. make drawings or letters.
5. get visited.
