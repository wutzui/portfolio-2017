About Me

My name is Yizzy.

I am a Taiwanese-based visual and interactive designer located in New York. Recently, I am preparing to graduate from MFA Design and Technology program at Parsons the New school of Design.

As being a visual designer with years of work experiences, I understand that interdisciplinary skills is essential for a designer who survive in this digital age. By working on projects in network functionality and front-end development, I am able to explore my passion of data and HCD interaction. I'm also studying in user experience that related to social issues, which I applied to my thesis research.

I believe that the best formula equal to great design is mass observation plus life experience. Therefore, making projects that truly help users and fulfill their needs is my conviction to puesue in the industry.
